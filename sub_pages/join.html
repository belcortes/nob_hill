Become a NHA Member!

Join the Nob Hill Association today! Support your neighborhood park! Learn about city issues that will affect your neighborhood and let your voice be heard. Enjoy the benefits of membership - discounts at local businesses, membership parties, opportunities to meet neighbors and make new friends! Join today! Click here, call or email info@nobhillassociation.org for more details. We look forward to welcoming you!

You can quickly recover the cost of your membership with the discounts you receive patronizing our business members! Funds raised by the Association have been used to make many improvements in Huntington Park. Over $1,250,000 has been raised and invested over the years in the complete restoration and maintenance of the Fountain of the Tortoises, for Park upgrades and maintenance, playground equipment, granite steps, bronze railings, perimeter walls and for improved lighting in Huntington Park. Help us to continue this good work! There is much more to come soon!
